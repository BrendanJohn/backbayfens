Park Rules

The Back Bay Fens is a public park located in the Emerald Necklace Park system of Boston. "Surrounded by some of Boston's leading educational, cultural and medical institutions, the Back Bay Fens is an eclectic mix of formal and community gardens, ball fields, memorials and historic structures. With places for passive recreation and active pursuits, the park offers a range of experiences such as gardening and sports and is a popular spot for birders."
